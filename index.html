Reflection

How can we, as future engineers, contribute to inclusivity and prevent prejudice in group projects, labs, or even future workplaces?

  • Actively hear every voice.
  • Not remaining silent in the face of offensive remarks or jokes
  • Encourage diversity-related projects

Building Ethical Engineering Environments

1. AwarenessRecognize bias and privilege
2. EducationLearn codes of ethics & legal rights
3. ActionIntervene when seeing misconduct and wrongdoings
4. InclusivityDesign workplaces for all
5. AccountabilityReflect, report, and improve policies

There is a culture built in engineering where everyone can feel safe, valued and heard. This in turn creates trust in engineers since they follow these ethically constructed rules.
